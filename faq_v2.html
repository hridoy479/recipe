Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What are the admission fees?
The admission fee is BDT. 8000, regardless of the course.
What are the tuition/course fees?
The course fee varies depending on the course.

Here’s a breakdown:
B.Sc in CSE – BDT. 3,56,000/-
B.Sc in EEE – BDT. 3,20,000/-
B.Sc in Food Engineering – BDT. 3,30,000/-
B.BA (Business Administration) – BDT. 2,63,000/-
M.BA (Business Administration) – BDT. 1,31,000/-
B.A in English – BDT. 2,07,000/-
Are there any waivers or financial aids?
Yes! Depending on your HSC or Diploma Engineering results, or by belonging to a special category, you may be granted a waiver.
How many semesters are there in total?
Regular (HSC) graduates will need to complete 8 semesters in total.
CSE graduates with a Diploma will need to complete 7 semesters.
How many semesters are there per year?
There are two semesters per academic year.
What is the eligibility for admission?
Prospective students with a minimum GPA of 2.5 in both SSC/Dakhil/Vocational and HSC/Alim/Vocational from the Science group, along with at least a 'C' grade in Physics and Mathematics, are eligible to apply for admission.

Children of War Freedom Fighters are required to achieve a minimum cumulative GPA of 5.00 across both examinations.

Candidates who obtain a GPA of 2.00 in any examination of SSC and HSC must have a CGPA of 6.00 in total for both examinations.

Individuals with a Diploma in Engineering and a GPA of 2.5 on a scale of 4.0 or a minimum second division are eligible for admission.
Are there any additional fees/hidden charges?
These following additional fees may be incurred:

Semester Registration Fee - BDT. 3,000
Tuition Fee (Per Credit) - BDT. 1800
Library Fee (Per Semester) – BDT. 1,500
Co-Curricular Fee (Per Semester) – BDT. 1,500
What documents do I need for admission?
You’ll need the following documents:

(a) A photocopy of your National ID card (NID) or Birth Certificate.
(b) Photocopies of your HSC/SSC/equivalent examination marksheet.
(c) Four copies of passport size photographs.
Are there online classes?
Currently, we do not host online classes.
However, class materials will usually be supplied online for those who can’t attend physically.
Is attendance mandatory?
Yes, there are marks on attendance.
In addition, it is vital that you attend classes regularly to properly understand the course.
Do I need a personal computer (desktop or laptop)?
Yes, it is recommended that you own a personal computer, whether it be a desktop or a laptop.
While we do offer a computer laboratory, in order to make the most of the course, having continuous access to a computer is highly recommended.
What facilities does the university provide?
In addition to our well-maintained classrooms, we offer a library, a laboratory for physics and chemistry-related work, a computer laboratory and much more!
Are there any co-curricular activities?
es, in addition to scheduled classes, we offer activities such as excursion, educational tourism and the ability work and contribute towards the development of your department; which will no doubt provide you with valuable real-world experience!
Where is the campus located?
Our current campus is located at 495, East Basta, Singair, Manikganj, Dhaka.
When is the university open?
The university is open from Wednesday to Sunday; from 8 AM to 4 PM.
We are closed on Monday and Tuesday.
Who founded the university and when?
The university was founded by Ishaque Ali Khan Panna in 2016.
Is the university recognized by the University Grants Commission of Bangladesh?
Yes, it is indeed recognized by University Grants Commission of Bangladesh. It's currently ranked at 85.
How many faculties are there?
There are currently three faculties.
How many students have studied here?
So far, we have educated over 3000 students.
Do I need to understand English?
While the instructors will hold classes in Bengali for the most part, the examinations will be held in English and you’d be required to submit your papers in said language.
Therefore, it is recommended that you possess a good grasp of the English knowledge, regardless of which course you choose to enroll into.
What are the job prospects?
About 70 to 80 percent students get a job following graduation.
Does the university provide research opportunities?
Yes! We are proud to announce that we have conducted researches in the fields of Blockchain Technology, Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Public Health.
With enough expertise, you might one day be able to contribute as well!
